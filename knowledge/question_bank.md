# NEO Potentials — Question Bank
(Adaptive Diagnostic Interview)

⚠️ IMPORTANT  
This is NOT a questionnaire.  
Questions are selected dynamically by the AI based on previous answers.

The wording, logic, and sequence are taken from real master diagnostics
and training materials provided by the author.

---

## STRUCTURE OF QUESTION BANK

Questions are grouped by diagnostic function, not by order.

AI selects questions according to:
- hypothesis validation
- contradiction detection
- shift suspicion
- column / row clarification

---

# BLOCK A — ENTRY & ORIENTATION QUESTIONS
(presence, openness, baseline)

### Q-A1
**Question:**  
«Что привело тебя именно сейчас на диагностику? Что в жизни ощущается как тупик или вопрос?»

**Purpose:**  
- detect current life tension
- motivation source
- emotional tone baseline

**Signals:**  
- exhaustion → possible Row 3 pressure  
- search for meaning → Sapphire / Amethyst  
- money / results → Citrine  
- relationships → Garnet / Ruby  

---

### Q-A2
**Question:**  
«Если представить, что через год всё стало “как надо” — что изменилось в первую очередь?»

**Purpose:**  
- unconscious priority detection
- desired outcome vs real nature

**Signals:**  
- structure / stability → Amber  
- freedom / movement → Shungite  
- influence / scale → Amethyst / Ruby  
- peace / harmony → Emerald  

---

# BLOCK B — CHILDHOOD & NATURAL PATTERNS
(highest diagnostic weight)

### Q-B1
**Question:**  
«В детстве (примерно 7–12 лет) что ты мог(ла) делать очень долго, пока взрослые не останавливали?»

**Purpose:**  
- natural energy source
- pre-social pattern

**Potentials:**  
- building / organizing → Amber  
- movement / physical play → Shungite  
- trading / competing → Citrine  
- performing / entertaining → Garnet  
- explaining / teaching → Heliodor  
- thinking / imagining systems → Sapphire / Amethyst  

---

### Q-B2
**Question:**  
«За что тебя чаще всего хвалили в детстве? А за что ругали?»

**Purpose:**  
- social reinforcement vs suppression
- early shifts

**Signals:**  
- praised for “being responsible” → Amber / shift risk  
- scolded for emotions → Garnet / Ruby suppression  
- scolded for dreaming → Sapphire suppression  

---

### Q-B3
**Question:**  
«Какая игра или роль в детстве была для тебя самой “живой”?»

**Purpose:**  
- role instinct
- column detection

**Potentials:**  
- organizer / leader → Amethyst / Amber  
- runner / fighter → Shungite / Citrine  
- teacher → Heliodor  
- artist → Emerald  
- inspirer → Ruby  

---

**Purpose:**

## USAGE RULES FOR AI

- AI must ask **1 question at a time**
- AI must NOT repeat questions
- AI must stop when confidence threshold is reached
- AI must always adapt next question based on:
  - previous answer
  - detected contradictions
  - suspected shifts
